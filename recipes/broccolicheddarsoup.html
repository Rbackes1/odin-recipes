Broccoli Cheddar Soup

There is nothing quite like a hot bowl of broccoli cheddar soup on a cold rainy day. I was first introduced to this wonderful creation in middle school believe it or not! That's when I fell in love with it; my growing teenage body couldn't get enough of the stuff at school. It's not like school food is very good either, so that should give you a hint as to how good this soup really is.

What You'll Need:

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups chiken stock
  • 1 1/2 cups roughly chopped broccoli florets
  • 1 cup matchsticl-cut carrots
  • 1 stalk celery, thinly sliced
  • 2 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste

Put It Together!

  1. Add 1 tablespoon butter to skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion and saute until translucent,about 5 minutes, set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan over medium-low, whisk together 1/4 cup melted butter and flour. Continue to whisk and cook 3 to 4 minutes. Add 1 to 2 tablespoons of milk to keep the flour from burning if necessary
  3. While whisking, slowly pour milk into flour mixture. Stir chicken stock into milk mixture. Bring to a simmer, cook until thickened, about 20 minutes. Add broccoli, carrots, sauteed onion, and celery, simmer until tender, about 20 minutes.
  4. Stir in cheese to saucepan until it melts, season with salt and pepper to taste.

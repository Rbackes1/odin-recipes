Moms Chili

A perfect meal to warm your bones on a chili day!

My mom would make this recipe starting early in the fall and all throught the colder winter months, even a few times in the summer because it was just that good. It has a great texture, right inbetween soup and stew contistency, perfect meat/bean/vegetable ratio, and just the right amount to spice to get your tounge dancing. This recipe is always just waiting to be used in the back of my head. It's so easy you could learn to code while you make it!

What You'll Need:

  • Ground beef, 1 lb
  • Green pepper, 1 diced
  • Yellow onion, 1 diced
  • Jalapeno pepper, 3 diced
  • 28 oz can dark red kidney beans, 1
  • 12 oz can chili beans, 2
  • chili powder,

Put it together!

  1. Sweat the diced onions in a large pot until fragrant
  2. Add the beef to the pot and brown
  3. Once browned, add the diced green peppers, jalapeno peppers, kidney beans, chili beans, chili powder and stir.
  4. Bring the chili soup up to boil, lower the heat, and cover. Simmer for 60 to 90 minutes.
  5. Top with anything your heart desires. Cheese, sour cream, tortilla strips, and so on.

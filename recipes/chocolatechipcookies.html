The Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Who Doesn't love a fresh, warm, full-of-love chocolate chip cookie? Well these cookies are going to knock your socks off beacuse they're the best you can get, and they're super easy to make!

What You'll Need:

  • 1 cup butter, Softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoon hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Put It Together!

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in eggs 1 at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  3. Dissolve baking soda in hot water, add to batter with salt
  4. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts
  5. drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pan(s)
  6. Bake for 10 minutes or until edges are browned
